SRM UNIVERSTY


SRM University, Sonipat (Haryana) is a leading private university established by the Haryana State Legislature under the Haryana Private Universities Act, 2006, and is part of the renowned SRM Group of Institutions. Recognized by the UGC and approved by bodies like BCI and AIU, the university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines including engineering, law, management, commerce, sciences, and liberal arts. With a modern campus spread over 47 acres, SRM Sonipat provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, smart classrooms, libraries, hostels, sports facilities, and industry-oriented training. The university emphasizes research, innovation, and global exposure through collaborations with leading international universities. Known for its strong placement support, skill development programs, and holistic student growth, SRM University Sonipat is committed to nurturing future-ready professionals and leaders.

KR MANGALAM



K.R. Mangalam University (KRMU), located in Gurgaon, is a leading institution known for its innovative teaching methods, modern infrastructure, and focus on holistic student development. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines like Engineering, Management, Law, Humanities, Sciences, and more, with special emphasis on emerging fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Machine Learning. KRMU provides state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, industry collaborations, and global exposure through international tie-ups, ensuring students are career-ready. Along with academics, the university encourages extracurricular activities, sports, cultural fests, and skill-development programs, creating a vibrant campus life. With its commitment to excellence, research, and innovation, KRMU nurtures students into confident, skilled, and responsible professionals for the future.
